Services We Offer

Lakshan Engineering provides expert Lathe, Milling, and Welding work for precise and durable metal fabrication. Our Cutter Grinding restores tools to peak sharpness, while Hydraulic and Power Steering Hose Repairs ensure reliable system performance.

Lathe Work

We provide precision lathe machining services for turning, facing, threading, and custom part fabrication to meet your exact specifications.

Milling Work

Our milling services offer high-accuracy metal shaping and surface finishing for components of all sizes using advanced milling machines.

Welding Work

We specialize in high-quality welding for repairs and fabrication, including MIG, TIG, and arc welding on a variety of metal types.

Cutter Grinding

Restore sharpness and performance with our professional blade grinding service, ensuring clean cuts and extended tool life.

Hydraulic Hose & Power Steering Hose Repair

We offer fast and reliable repairs and replacements for hydraulic and power steering hoses to ensure your systems run smoothly and safely.
